In summary, our contributions are,

- We introduce a one-stream transformer of multi-stage from Swin Transformer for tracking, with feature extraction and template-search relation modeling simultaneously.
- We develop a transformer-based tracking architecture based upon Swin Transformer layer. It allows template and search interact in a local window, which can induce the interference part and enhance the interaction feature to a certain degree.
- We carry out comprehensive experiments to explore the superior performance and inference speed of one-stream muti-stage framework over two-stream two-stage or one-stream one-stage architecture.
- multi-stage correlation, windows-attention, gradually add field receptive (competitive for small objects)
